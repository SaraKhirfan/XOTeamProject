  • on going

    Organism: Arabidopsis thaliana
    Mission:Veggie Plant Growth System
    Outcomes:
    Investigated plant growth in microgravity; contributed to understanding plant biology in space.

    DataLink: GeneLab

  • 2010

    Organism: Arabidopsis thaliana seedlings
    Mission:ISS / STS-130 & Expedition 22 (using the EMCS facility)
    Outcomes:
    Revealed how red-light and blue-light phototropic responses operate under microgravity; isolated the red-light phototropism pathway without gravitational bias.

    DataLink: GeneLab

  • 2015-2018

    Organism: Microbes, organic compounds, cosmic dust
    Mission:ISS / Exposed Facility on JEM (Kibo)
    Outcomes:
    Demonstrated survival / degradation curves of microbes & organics in real space vacuum/UV; captured interplanetary dust & micrometeoroids, tested aerogel dust capture; provided data relevant to panspermia hypotheses.

    DataLink: GeneLab

His legacy continues to inspire new generations in exploring biology beyond Earth.
