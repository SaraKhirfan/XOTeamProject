About LEVIN

LEVIN is named in tribute to Wolf V. LEVIN, a pioneering scientist in space biology whose work continues to inspire the search for life beyond Earth. The name also honors the Martian crater LEVIN, a symbol of exploration and discovery on another world. Our logo reflects this connection, representing Mars and its landscapes, and stands as a reminder of our mission: exploring the biology of space.

Meet Our Team

XO Team, founded in late 2024, is a student-led initiative representing the University of Jordan. Comprised of talented students, the team focuses on creating innovative web and mobile solutions, embracing collaboration and continuous learning in every project. In a short time, XO Team has completed notable projects, earned recognition in local and international competitions, and organized workshops to share skills and knowledge, reflecting its commitment to innovation and professional growth.
