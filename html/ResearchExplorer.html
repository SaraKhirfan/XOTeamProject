4-he NASA Performance Assessment Workstation: cognitive performance during head-down bed rest

Head-down bed rest is used to simulate effects of microgravity on the human body. This study examines how cognitive performance changes under those conditions. PubMed Useful for understanding what effects space travel might have on cognition, attention, memory, etc., and how to plan countermeasures
